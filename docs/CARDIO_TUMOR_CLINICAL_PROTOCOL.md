# Advanced Onco-Cardiology Protocol: SFWB in Mass Tumor Resection Operations

This clinical framework governs the use of SFWB during large-scale tumor resections complicated by cardiovascular disease, supporting the *cardio_tumor_journal.pdf* research data.

## 1. Clinical Rationale in Onco-Cardiology
Patients with advanced tumors who require complex surgeries often suffer from severe anemia or blood loss. Traditional donor blood transfusions can sometimes cause unintended immune responses or increase the risk of systemic inflammation. 

SFWB provides a non-immunogenic, plant-derived oxygen transport matrix that delivers vital oxygen to tissues without triggering these donor-related immune pathways.
